Trend Pullback (TREND_PB)

An intraday NIFTY option-buying strategy that waits for an established trend, lets it pull back to value, and enters only on a confirmed resumption — designed to buy into strength, not to guess reversals.

What it does, in one line

Establish a 15-minute trend bias → wait for a healthy 5-minute pullback into the moving-average zone → enter a slightly-ITM option only when a resumption candle confirms the trend is continuing. Every exit rides on the spot (index) price, not the option premium, except one premium-based disaster backstop.

Why this design

  • Price structure first, indicators second. The trend is defined by higher-highs / higher-lows (or lower-lows / lower-highs), with EMAs and VWAP only as health filters — not as the trigger.
  • No volume input — by design. The NIFTY index has no real tradable volume. Instead of a fake volume proxy, entry conviction is measured by candle body vs ATR (a resumption candle must have a body ≥ 0.5× the recent ATR).
  • Exits are weighted over entries. A structural stop protects capital, breakeven removes risk early, and an ATR chandelier trail lets winners run — the right tail is where option-buying makes its money.
  • No fixed target, no partial booking. Partials cap the winners that pay for all the small losers, so the full position rides the trail.
  • Binary gates, not confidence scores. A small parameter budget (~7 real knobs) keeps the strategy from over-fitting to noise.

Entry — all conditions must pass, in order

① 15m trend bias → ② healthy 5m pullback → ③ resumption candle → ④ gates pass → buy slightly-ITM option

① 15-minute trend bias (uses only completed 15m bars)

  • Structure: last confirmed swing high > prior swing high AND last swing low > prior swing low (higher-high + higher-low for an uptrend; mirror for down).
  • Health filters: EMA20(15m) > EMA50(15m), EMA20 sloping in the trend direction, and spot on the correct side of the session-anchored VWAP.

② Healthy 5-minute pullback

  • Price pulls back into the EMA20(5m) zone — a real pause, not a one-bar wick.
  • Depth cap: the pullback extreme (anchored to the swing peak/trough, not the whole window) stays within TREND_PB_PULLBACK_MAX_ATR × ATR5 — rejects deep, broken pullbacks that are really reversals.

③ Resumption candle — the trigger

  • A 5m candle closes beyond the prior bar's high (low for shorts) AND reclaims EMA20(5m).
  • Body ≥ TREND_PB_BODY_ATR_MULT × ATR5 (default 0.5) — the conviction proxy that replaces volume.
  • Entry is on the close, never on a wick.

④ Gates (shared infrastructure)

  • Inside the entry window (TREND_PB_ENTRY_START–TREND_PB_ENTRY_END).
  • VIX in band (optional, TREND_PB_VIX_ENABLED), bid-ask spread OK, optional OI-buildup gate, and daily loss / max-trades / loss-streak not tripped.

Exits — first hit wins (all on SPOT except the premium backstop)

No fixed profit target and no partial booking — both deliberate. Position size is fixed lots.

Option selection

Buys a slightly-ITM CE (uptrend) or PE (downtrend) via the shared instrument resolver, shifted TREND_PB_ITM_STEPS strikes in-the-money (default 1 ≈ delta 0.6). Premium band and bid-ask spread are checked before entry; premium is polled every ~3s while in a position. Runs on the shared Fyers tick feed.

Live safety — triple-gated

A real Fyers order can fire only when all three are true: TREND_PB_LIVE_ENABLED=true and global LIVE_HARNESS_DRY_RUN=false and TREND_PB_LIVE_DRY_RUN=false — plus a valid Fyers login. Out of the box it is dry-run: it logs the order it would place and sends nothing. Recommended path: watch it on paper, confirm Live decisions match Paper on a recorded /replay session, then consider flipping the gates.

Build status

All three phases shipped: Paper (canonical engine + UI), Backtest (walk-forward + baseline + cost haircut), and Live (via harness, dry-run by default). Crash-recovery of an open live position across a server restart is a documented deferral (matches ORB) and is not built yet.

Trend Pullback is independent of every other strategy — it shares only the single Fyers tick feed and the common guard/charge/instrument infrastructure. ORB and all other strategies are untouched by it.
