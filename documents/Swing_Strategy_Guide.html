01 — Strategy Overview

A deliberately simple, chart-driven swing strategy. Three indicators decide entries, and the stop is the previous candle's extreme, trailed candle-by-candle. The same getSignal() drives Backtest, Paper, Live, Replay and the Live Harness, so all modes agree by construction.

In one line

CE = RSI in band & price at/above EMA21 & SAR below price. PE = mirror. Stop = previous candle low (CE) / high (PE), trailed each candle. Exits: SL · breakeven · option-stop · opposite signal · exit-before-close · EOD.

There is no strength tier, no ADX, no EMA9/EMA30, no candle-body or SAR-distance gate, and no 3-tier trail. Those were all removed in the 2026-05-27 redefinition (see section 12).

04 — Entry Logic — 3 Conditions

Re-evaluated on every live tick while flat (intra-candle). All three conditions must be true. The EMA21 test fires whether the candle is already on the correct side of EMA21 or crossing it now.

BUY_CE — long call
Bullish
1. RSI_CE_MIN < RSI < RSI_CE_MAX (above momentum floor, below overbought cap)
2. Price at/above EMA21 → candle.high ≥ EMA21
3. SAR below the candle → SAR.trend = 1
→ Initial SL = previous candle LOW
BUY_PE — long put
Bearish
1. RSI_PE_MIN < RSI < RSI_PE_MAX (above oversold floor, below momentum cap)
2. Price at/below EMA21 → candle.low ≤ EMA21
3. SAR above the candle → SAR.trend = -1
→ Initial SL = previous candle HIGH
Defaults: RSI_CE_MIN=52, RSI_CE_MAX=80, RSI_PE_MAX=48, RSI_PE_MIN=20. The overbought/oversold caps stop you chasing exhausted moves. Entry price = current tick LTP.

05 — Stop Loss & Trailing

Previous-candle trailing stop

Initial SL = the prior completed candle's low (CE) / high (PE), used as-is — no cap, no floor.

Trail: at every candle close, tighten the SL to that just-closed candle's low (CE) / high (PE). Tighten-only — it never loosens. Enforced intra-candle (tick-by-tick) during the next bar; in Live it is also pushed to the broker hard-SL.

Breakeven: once price is +BREAKEVEN_PTS in favour, SL jumps to entry (tighten-only).

Because entry happens right at EMA21 and the previous candle's extreme often sits a few points away, the stop is structurally tight — expect frequent small stop-outs, especially on 3-min. Tighter timeframes amplify this.

06 — Exit Logic

Checked per tick (price stops) and per candle close (signal/time stops):

#ExitRule
1Prev-candle SL hitCE: ltp ≤ SL · PE: ltp ≥ SL
2Option-premium stopExit if option LTP ≤ entryLtp × (1 − OPT_STOP_PCT)
3BreakevenAfter SL has moved to entry, a pullback to entry exits flat
4Opposite signalHolding CE and a BUY_PE fires → exit (and vice-versa)
5Exit before closeSquare off any open position at SWING_EOD_EXIT_TIME (default 15:15)
6EOD auto-stopAt TRADE_STOP_TIME (15:30) — square off + stop the engine
7Time-stopFlat trade bleeding theta closed via tradeGuards.checkTimeStop
In Backtest there is no live option LTP, so the option-stop is approximated as an equivalent adverse spot move (OPT_STOP_PCT × est-premium ÷ delta); it is disabled for futures.

07 — Same-Side Cooldown

After an SL hit or option-stop on a side, new entries on that side are blocked for SWING_SL_PAUSE_CANDLES candles (default 3). The other side is free. Set to 0 to disable. This prevents immediately re-entering the same losing setup tick-after-tick.

11 — Expiry & Live Gating

  • 0DTE refusal: live/paper /start is blocked when the configured expiry == today. Replay bypasses this (force=1) so historical sessions aren't aborted.
  • TRADE_EXPIRY_DAY_ONLY — when on, only trade on NIFTY expiry day.
  • SWING_OPTION_EXPIRY_OVERRIDE / SWING_OPTION_EXPIRY_TYPE let Swing run a different expiry from the other strategies; blank inherits the common expiry.
  • Live orders are double-gated: SWING_LIVE_ENABLED=true and global LIVE_HARNESS_DRY_RUN=false, with a per-strategy SWING_LIVE_DRY_RUN override to keep Swing simulated while others trade live.

12 — Removed vs Old Strategy

The 2026-05-27 redefinition removed all of the following (and their Settings fields):

EMA9 touch EMA30 trend gate ADX filter candle-body filter min/max SAR-distance Logic-3 SAR-lag overrides STRONG / MARGINAL tiers 3-tier trailing hybrid initial-SL cap prev-candle structural trail 50% candle rule near-miss N/8 audit

Entry is now always intra-candle (no STRONG/MARGINAL timing split), and the stop is purely the previous candle's extreme + breakeven + option-stop. See SWING.md in the repo for the code-level reference.
